Standard Operating Procedure – Access Management

Objective:
To ensure secure, role-based access to all functions within the TrialTrack platform.

Policy:
- Admins can add/remove users and assign roles.
- Researchers can submit trial data but cannot edit others’ submissions.
- Viewers have read-only access.

Procedure:
1. New users are registered via the admin dashboard.
2. Passwords must be 12+ characters with alphanumeric + symbols.
3. Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all roles.
4. Access logs are retained for 2 years.

Review Cycle: Every 6 months
